001

Stone 002 · Mateo Cantú · Manufacturing, Welder

002
Mateo Cantú · Manufacturing · Welder Provisional · External validator pending · Carmen Solís / Lincoln Electric · 2026-08-12

Annika, you asked on May 26 — the day we met — what happens when a senior welder retires and takes forty years of undocumented knowledge with him. That question became the actual blocking question for this stone, not background context.

Mateo is a seventeen-year welder at the Phoenix plant. The graph detected consistent shift-handoff completion, a training sequence in certified welding procedures, and a documented knowledge-transfer session from René Fuentes, who retired in June. The artifact record is real. The question Cairn’s architecture surfaces for a case like this: who holds the external domain expertise to validate the skill node? For welding certifications at this level, Cordova’s internal attesters are not the right final authority.

The architecture has a deferred-attestation path for exactly this reason. The stone is on the ledger tonight as provisional: visible to Inez and to you, excluded from compensation logic, promotion-eligibility logic, and any automated downstream decision until Carmen Solís of Lincoln Electric signs. Carmen’s site visit is 2026-08-12. Until her attestation lands, this stone does not move. The ledger marks it clearly; it cannot be used in a personnel decision in its current state.

The C-2 connector took 19 days instead of the 12 Yield had estimated. Cordova’s shift-handoff app needed a schema cleanup — the handoff records across plants were not consistently keyed. Andre Sosa shipped the fix; the connector went live without further friction. The revised estimate held. No drama, but a schedule miss belongs in the record.

Carolina Ruiz caught one factual error in the ledger draft during the pre-publication review: the SyncSet was for the inventory service; the draft had written “orders service.” Wrong service. She caught it before publication. We corrected the record. The provenance chain reflects the correction. This is exactly why the review step runs before the entry publishes.

The next checkpoint is Carmen Solís on August 12. I will write again then. If anything here is unclear before that date, Office Hours runs every Tuesday at 14:00 PT, or reply directly.
