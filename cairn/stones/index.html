The stones.

Every skill attested on a Cairn customer's people-graph is recorded here, in order, forever. The ledger is append-only: entries are never removed, never silently amended. Corrections are logged as new entries with a reference to the original. This page exists because Cairn makes consequential claims about real people, and the people those claims concern — and the customers who act on them — deserve a public record with a traceable chain of evidence behind every single stone. If a customer ends their Stratum relationship, their stones remain on this ledger in perpetuity, per the 45-day termination clause in the Cairn design-partner MOU. The methodology behind each stone is queryable. The agents involved are named. The humans who attested are named. The ones who reviewed are named. The ones who found errors are named and thanked.

Reflection · Tessera · W08 D5

The first stone was easy by design. We chose Elena's story as the opening because it is complete: detection, recommendation, delivery, verification, attestation, correction — every link in the chain closed before the page went up. A clean arc. We needed at least one of those, to show what the full chain looks like when everything works.

The second stone was the test. Esteban Morales retired; the certifying human is gone. The honest move was to put the provisional stone on the same page, same day, with the same design weight. The easy one alone would have been a marketing artifact. The hard one alone would have been an apology. They belong together.

Annika reviewed both stones before publication. Mateo and Elena saw their own entries first — not as a courtesy, but because they are the people whose working lives these stones represent. Carolina Ruiz caught one error; Tomás Herrera caught one error. Both corrections are documented in the entries above. A ledger that hides corrections is not a ledger.

The next stones we publish will be more compact than these two. The detail here is the founding pattern — the proof that every link is queryable, every human in the loop is named, every limitation is disclosed. Once that pattern is understood, subsequent stones can be compact rows: ID, skill, subject, date, badge. The chain behind each row will be identical in structure to the chain you just read.
