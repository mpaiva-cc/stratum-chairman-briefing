§ 05   What we are

Stratum is not a system of record. Stratum is the system of truth — a unified people graph over the systems you already have.

No rip-and-replace. No 18-month implementation. No SI partner required.

§ 06   The product

Three pillars.
One copilot.

i — Substrate

The people graph

A composable layer that unifies the fifty source systems an enterprise already runs — identity, comp, performance, recruiting, equity, payroll — into a single queryable truth.

ii — Interface

The copilot

An agentic interface CHROs can talk to in plain language. Asks better questions back. Drafts the board memo. Runs the comp-band exception. Always within policy.

iii — Output

Decisions with receipts

Every recommendation cites its sources, surfaces its assumptions, and writes back a versioned record. The audit trail is the product — not an afterthought.
