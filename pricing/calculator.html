§ The fine print · Read this before you forward the number

A first-pass estimate is not a quote.

This calculator is a first-pass estimate. Your real cost depends on your specific systems, your peak usage, your geographic footprint, the shape of your write-back requirements, and any custom work your team asks us to scope. The number above is what to budget — not what to sign.

All quotes are 12-month minimum. The platform fee bills annual prepaid; the usage tier bills quarterly in arrears against the decisions the Console actually supported and your users actually accepted. Annual prepay gets a 20% effective discount — we can do a 2-year commit for a slightly larger discount, and we are willing to talk about a revenue-share structure on the write-back module if your CFO prefers that shape.

Procurement timeline: most customers complete MSA + DPA in 21–31 days from first quote. The fastest we have signed is 8 days (a buyer who had seen an AI rider before, and a General Counsel who reads contracts on Saturdays). Talk to procurement@stratum.ai if you need a faster path or a non-standard shape.
