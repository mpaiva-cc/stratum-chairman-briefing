We never publish per-seat pricing because we don't price per seat. Annual contracts are 12-month minimum; the platform fee is billed annual prepaid, the usage tier (below) is billed quarterly in arrears against the decisions the Console actually supported and your users actually accepted.

§ 02 · The usage tier, explained

A decision is the unit of value. So we charge for it.

"Every CHRO we've talked to spends seventy percent of her week in reporting and thirty percent in people. We sell back the seventy percent. The unit of value is a decision the Console supported with a defensible answer. We charge for that."

A decision, to us, is a specific event: a question answered with a citable cohort of n ≥ 10 employees, with provenance back to the source systems, and accepted by a named user in the Console (or written back into a downstream system through a Decisions module). It is not a prompt. It is not a query. It is the moment the answer becomes a decision.

We do this because per-seat pricing rewards us for the wrong thing — getting more humans into a tool — and per-query pricing punishes you for exploring. Pricing the decision aligns the line item to the work. When your CHRO answers fewer questions because the platform is doing the reporting for her, your bill goes down. We want that.

§ 03 · What's not included

What we don't sell, and why we'll tell you so.

A contract is a piece of trust. The fastest way to break it is to leave something off the page that should have been on it. Three things Stratum does not yet do — read these before you decide.

No. 01 · Write-back coverage

We do not yet write back to every system you run.

Today, Stratum writes back to Workday HRIS, Greenhouse, and Lattice. Coming Q4 2026: ADP-WFN, Carta, Okta governance. Coming H1 2027: SAP SuccessFactors, Oracle HCM, UKG Pro write paths. Read-only ingestion is available today for all eight on the roadmap.

No. 02 · System of record

We are not your system of record.

Stratum is the system of truth that sits on top of the systems of record you already pay for. We don't run payroll, we don't hold the employee file, we don't replace Workday. We resolve them into one defensible graph and give your CHRO a copilot on top.

No. 03 · Geography

We do not currently sell outside US and EMEA HQs.

Stratum is generally available for US-headquartered and EMEA-headquartered companies today. APAC HQ is H1 2027, with Singapore data residency and a Tokyo regional engineer. If your HQ is in APAC and you want to be the first design partner there, the email is at the bottom of this page.
