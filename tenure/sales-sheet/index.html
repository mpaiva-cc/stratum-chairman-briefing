The third Stratum product

Every employee event, handled as a decision.

Joiner. Mover. Leaver. Anniversary. Promotion. Parental leave. Return. Retirement. Each one is a moment in your people graph — and now each one has an agent, a boundary, and a defense. Stratum Tenure rides on top of your system of record and ships with three new agents bounded by the decision-class the Compact ratified in Briefing 014.

§ The problem

Onboarding is one moment. Employee events are a category.

  • At 100,000 employees, joiners are 5.6% of lifecycle events. The other 94.4% — internal moves, anniversaries, leaves, exits — sit untouched by legacy HCM modules.
  • IT provisioning fails on Day 1 outside Rippling. Paperwork hell remains. Buddy systems fizzle by week 3.
  • Reporting is post-hoc: retention at 90 days, not leading indicators.
§ The solution · three new agents
Threshold
The line you cross

Owns the irreversible writes. Pre-board through Day 30 plus termination. Equipment, identity, paperwork, payroll, asset reclaim. The agent we trust with regulated forms because the boundary is written down.

Lattice
The structure you join

Owns the relational moments. Buddy, mentor, manager check-ins, 30/60/90 plans, team intros. Reversible, social-graph aware, low regulatory surface.

Ember
The small warmth

Owns recognition. Anniversaries, milestones, promotion announcements, retirement notes. Lowest stakes by failure budget; highest stakes by brand voice.

§ Compliance posture
  • I-9 / E-Verify: Threshold prepares Section 1; a human signs Section 2. DHS Section 2 cannot be delegated. Ever.
  • EU AI Act Annex III: employment decisions in scope from launch; the decision-class taxonomy and audit log meet the human-oversight requirement.
  • GDPR · CCPA · NYC AEDT · state pay-transparency: jurisdiction-aware templates. Bring-your-own-cloud option for EU offices.
  • SOC 2 Type II: existing scope extends to the PII vault subsystem. Coalfire audit window scheduled.
§ Decision class · four tiers · one rule

Agents act when the worst case is "we apologize and reissue." Humans sign when the worst case is "we get sued, fined, or made the news." Encoded as a workflow predicate, not a config flag.

DecideReversible within 24h, non-regulated. Equipment, standard IdP, buddy assignment, anniversary acknowledgements.
RecommendHuman approves with one click. IdP elevated scopes, role start-date confirm, payroll record draft, departure timing.
SuggestAgent drafts; human signs. Offer-letter copy, promotion announcements, 30/60/90 plans, retirement notes.
Read-onlyAgent surfaces, cannot act. I-9 Section 2 verification, E-Verify submission, EEOC adverse-impact reports.
