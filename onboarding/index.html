Stratum / Onboarding · The Threshold view · Day 0 → Day 30

The first thirty days, handled.

Onboarding is one moment in a longer story. This page is the Day-0 view — the surface of Stratum Tenure where Threshold, the irreversible-writes agent, runs from offer accept through Day 30.

Why this page exists The HCM category calls this onboarding. We call the whole product Tenure — onboarding, transfers, promotions, leaves, anniversaries, exits. This page covers the joiner slice. The agent, the boundary, and the failure budget below apply to all of it.

What Threshold actually does.

Threshold owns the irreversible writes between offer-accept and Day-30. It reads from your system of record (Workday, BambooHR, ADP, Rippling) and orchestrates the downstream surfaces — IdP, equipment, e-signature, background check, payroll, badge.

It is allowed to act when the worst-case outcome is reversible within 24 hours. It is allowed to recommend when a human can approve with one click. It is allowed to read from regulated forms (I-9, E-Verify, EEO-1 self-ID) and surface what it sees — but it cannot submit them. The boundary is written down. Read the charter.

If we had to make this product as one sentence: the agent gets the laptop on the desk, the Slack on the laptop, the buddy in the calendar, and the manager unblocked — without making any decision the regulators care about.
