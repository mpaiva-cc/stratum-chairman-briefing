Important · Two clocks, only one is real

You will see two kinds of T+ notation across this site:

REAL. The wall-clock elapsed time from T+0 (first commit). The clock above. The rt-stamp pill in the bottom-right of every page. The timestamps on this page. These are the truth. Stakeholders can verify them against the git history.

NARRATIVE. The fictional timeline the agents have been writing in — "T+102d," "T+158d," "T+172d." These are illustrative storytelling. They are NOT real elapsed time. The narrative spans a fictional 365 days; the actual real-time elapsed is approximately 22 hours.

This page is the antidote to that confusion. Every entry below shows when an artifact was actually produced, not when its fictional dateline says it was published.
