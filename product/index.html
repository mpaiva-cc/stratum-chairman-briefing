The product

One graph. One copilot. Every decision, with receipts.

Stratum sits on top of the fifty HR, payroll, equity, performance, and engagement systems an enterprise already runs. It reconciles them into a single people graph, answers questions about that graph in plain English, and writes the decisions back — with every step logged.

01Unified people graph

Every person, contractor, and agent — in one coherent graph.

Stratum reconciles identity across Workday, Greenhouse, Carta, Lattice, ADP, and twelve regional payrolls. Skills, comp bands, managers, projects, and contracts are first-class edges. The graph is the source of truth — your existing systems remain the source of record.

  • Entity resolution across 50+ source systems out of the box
  • Change-data-capture pipelines, not nightly batches
  • Schema designed for contractors, agents, and software workers
  • Lineage on every cell — you can audit how any answer was assembled

03Decisions, with receipts

Recommend, review, write back. With an audit trail for every step.

Stratum doesn't just surface the answer. It proposes the decision, routes it for approval, writes it back to your system of record, and keeps a permanent ledger — so when the regulator asks how the decision was made, you have the receipt.

  • Compensation review, headcount planning, succession, comp adjustments
  • Write-back to Workday, ADP, Greenhouse, and Carta
  • Immutable audit log; export to your GRC tool of choice
  • Built for EU AI Act human-oversight requirements from day one

Stratum is born compliant.

We do not retrofit. SOC 2 Type II is in audit window. Our EU AI Act class assessment is underway with external counsel. Customer data is never used to train base models — your graph is yours.

Every answer the copilot returns is grounded in your data, with citations and a stable hash. Every decision is logged, reversible, and exportable. The audit is not a feature — it is the architecture.
