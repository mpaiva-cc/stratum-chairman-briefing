Executive Summaries

One screen. What changed. Why it matters. Recommended decision. Team ownership. Reserved for events the Compact must see today.

Executive summaries are event-driven, not scheduled. They exist for the cases where a daily brief would arrive too slowly. The bar is high — false-positive exec summaries erode the channel — so the format is reserved for market shifts, regulatory developments, and competitive threats where the Compact needs to make a decision within the week.

Standard structure

  • What changedOne paragraph.
  • Why it mattersStratum-specific impact.
  • Risk / opportunityBoth sides, named.
  • Recommended decisionConcrete, with a default if no decision is made.
  • Team ownershipWhich Stratum agent owns the follow-up.
