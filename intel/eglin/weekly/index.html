Weekly Research Digests

A Monday rollup of the HCM industry — trends, competitor moves, AI shifts, compliance updates, customer pain points, recommended actions.

Every Monday Eglin publishes a 1,200–2,000-word digest covering eight beats: market trends, competitor moves, AI developments, compliance updates, customer pain points, product opportunities, UX implications, and recommended actions. The digest is dense by design — leadership reads it before the week's first decision meeting.

The eight beats

  • Market trendsDemand, hiring volume, capital flows, M&A, layoffs, executive moves.
  • Competitor movesLaunches, repositioning, pricing changes, customer wins/losses, agent and AI feature shipments.
  • AI developmentsNew model releases relevant to HCM, evaluation/safety research, agent frameworks, vendor AI claims.
  • Compliance updatesEU AI Act, NYC AEDT, state pay-transparency, ADA/WCAG case law, biometric data laws.
  • Customer pain pointsG2, Capterra, Reddit, LinkedIn, podcast and conference signals — patterns, not anecdotes.
  • Product opportunitiesWhere Stratum could differentiate. Cross-signal patterns the Compact should consider.
  • UX implicationsInteraction patterns Tessera should learn from or counter-design.
  • Recommended actionsRouted to a specific Stratum agent with a specific next step.
