00 · Headline read

Where Stratum can credibly win.

Gusto is the SMB payroll leader with strong brand and ~300,000 customers concentrated below 100 employees. The April 24, 2026 announcement of "75 new features" — including S-corp election with AI-guided advisory, ChatGPT/Claude/Slack-integrated AI workflows, the Gus AI assistant for HR tasks, and a Money Dashboard with real-time "safe-to-pay" payroll signal — is the strategic move of the year [1][2]. The Plus plan increasingly positions Gusto as a functional HRIS for the 10-100 employee range [1].

The Trustpilot 2.5/5 across 2,300+ reviews reveals the consistent pattern: users love the product when it works, struggle with support when it doesn't [3][4]. This is the SMB-trust hinge.

Not the direct Stratum competitive set. Gusto's ICP is below Stratum's. The realistic motion is upgrade-path partnership: Gusto customers growing past 100 employees and wanting agentic recruiting are the natural Stratum lane. Compass should have a "Stratum Recruiter for Gusto alumni" positioning page.

01 · Company overview

SMB payroll, expanding into HRIS.

Gusto was founded in 2011 in San Francisco. ~300,000 customers, concentrated below 100 employees. The product is SMB payroll first, with HRIS, benefits, time tracking, basic ATS layered. The April 2026 launch of 75 new features — Gus AI assistant, ChatGPT/Claude/Slack workflow integrations, Money Dashboard with safe-to-pay signal, S-corp election AI advisory — is a deliberate move to extend Gusto's product surface upward [1][2].

02 · Positioning

"Payroll, benefits, HR — built for small business."

Gusto positions on small-business friendliness — friendly UX, predictable pricing, expert support, increasingly AI-augmented operations. The 2026 framing extends to "AI-powered workflows" with the LLM-integration story.

03 · Target customers

SMB, US-only.

  • Core ICP: 1–100 employees, US-only, professional services, consultancies, restaurants, healthcare-adjacent.
  • Geography: US-only payroll natively.
04 · Product strengths

What Gusto actually does well.

  • SMB UX and ease of use. Best-in-class for the 1-50 employee tier. [3]
  • Gus AI assistant. In-product chat for HR Q&A, data pulls, time-off approvals [1].
  • LLM-integration workflows. ChatGPT, Claude, Slack — Gusto data connected via natural-language to external AI [1].
  • Money Dashboard with safe-to-pay signal. Real-time payroll-readiness indicator [1].
  • S-corp election AI advisory. Specific use-case AI for solopreneurs [1].
  • Brand strength in SMB. Strong customer reference network.
