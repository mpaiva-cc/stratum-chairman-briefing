Three programs. Narrow on purpose.

We don't run a tiered “bronze / silver / gold” gauntlet. There are three relationships Stratum knows how to support, and we'd rather do those well than pretend to do six. Pick the one that fits.

§ 04 · How partnerships work

Economics, and the operating model.

The economics are simple. We share 12% of first-year ARR with consulting partners on referred deals, on a 12-month cookie window. No deal-registration gymnastics; we honor the cookie. If your partner lead introduced the customer to Stratum and the customer signs within the year, the share is yours — regardless of who else walked the procurement process. After year one, the partnership relationship is renewed annually; the revenue share itself does not extend in perpetuity, which is intentional. We want to keep proving we deserve the relationship.

The operating model is just as simple. Every consulting partner has a named Stratum Partner Lead. Today that is Compass for the top accounts — a deliberate hands-on arrangement while the program is young. In Q4 2026 we hire a dedicated VP Partnerships, and the named lead handoff happens with full continuity. Quarterly partner reviews are on the calendar from day one. The first annual partner summit is March 2027, two days, in-person, by invitation. Small on purpose.

§ 05 · What we expect

What we expect from partners.

A short, honest list of partner obligations. None of these are negotiable, and we'd rather have a smaller partner roster that holds to all five than a larger one that holds to some.

  • § 01 · Training

    Partners must complete the 2-day technical training before recommending Stratum to a customer. We won't ship a referral fee to anyone who hasn't sat in the seat.

  • § 02 · Disclosure

    Partners must disclose the partnership relationship to customers. The customer should know that the consultant in the room has a revenue-share relationship with the platform being recommended. Always.

  • § 03 · Referral, not resale

    Partners cannot resell Stratum — we are not a reseller model. Stratum contracts directly with the customer. Partners refer. The economics are clean and the relationship with the customer stays direct.

  • § 04 · Pricing discipline

    Partners must respect Stratum's pricing discipline. We don't discount through partners. We don't run a side-door discount channel. Our price list is the price list; we believe in it; we hold it.

  • § 05 · No deceptive comparison

    Partners must respect Stratum's no-deceptive-comparison policy — we don't badmouth competitors and we don't ask our partners to. If a customer is better served by Workday or Rippling, we say so. Partners do the same.
