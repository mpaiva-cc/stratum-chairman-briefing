Why the conversion makes sense. Meridian's evaluation signed on T+114d covered three workflows their Director of People Operations, Friederike Berg, named on the discovery call: shift- coverage modelling for the maintenance crews; pay-band review against German collective-bargaining cycles; and a quarterly skills-inventory walkthrough that currently runs in Excel and takes their team a week. § 08 · The Greenhouse partnership question

The same week we ship the new product line, we call Daniel Chait

A short section. The honest sentence carried as the framing for the T+177d heads-up call.

Of the seven active customers we work with, four use Greenhouse as their ATS today; for the Console-only relationships, the Greenhouse connector is a daily surface for the customer-engineering team. The Greenhouse partnership is not a paper artifact — Stratum's Greenhouse connector is in the Greenhouse partner directory; their solutions team has co-presented with us at one customer in the last year; the relationship is operational.

What changes today. Roughly sixty percent of our Console customers will keep Greenhouse as the ATS they want to keep, and the Stratum-Greenhouse partnership continues to serve them. Roughly forty percent — and the three beta-cohort customers are inside this forty — have told us they would prefer a single integrated surface, and Stratum Recruiter is the surface we are building for them. At the ATS layer, for that forty percent, Stratum Recruiter is now a competitor to Greenhouse.

The call. Compass and Vector have a heads-up call scheduled with Daniel Chait directly on T+177d. The framing is: we are telling you before we told the market, because that is how the relationship works. The agents' position is that this is not a partnership-ending event — the joint customers continue to be joint customers; the connector continues to be maintained; the directory listing stays. We do not expect Daniel to receive it warmly on first hearing. We do expect that, given a fortnight to think about it, he will see the same picture we see — that the customers who chose Stratum Recruiter would have left Greenhouse anyway, and the customers who stay on Greenhouse are stronger customers because Stratum has been consistently honest with both sides.
