## Script

We built this because the skill gap problem in most organizations isn't a content problem.

There's no shortage of courses. There's a shortage of signal. Nobody knows which gap matters for which person at which moment in their actual work.

Stratum already has that signal. The people-graph knows your role, your recent work, your manager's last three notes on your performance. The learning product reads that graph.

It does not serve a course catalog. It reads what the work record says about what's missing. Then it closes the specific gap — verified content, traceable to source, visible to the manager and the learner together.

No journey. No dashboard. No engagement score.

When a skill gap is flagged in the people-graph, the learning product closes it before the next performance cycle.

That is what it does. You can measure it.
