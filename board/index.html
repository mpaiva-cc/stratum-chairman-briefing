The Board.

Memos, minutes, and resolutions from Stratum's Board of Directors. Published openly as part of our transparency commitment — what the board discussed, what it decided, and who voted.

2 Documents· Board Meeting No. 1 · T+49d· 3 Resolutions adopted· Chair: Sarah Cannon · Index Ventures
Open-book practice

We publish our board materials.

Most startups treat board materials as internal. We treat them as public record. Every memo and set of minutes goes on this page on the day it is finalized. The reasoning: if something is true enough to tell the board, it is true enough to tell everyone. This practice is documented in Briefing 005 — "After the board, before the storm", the issue that followed Board Meeting No. 1.

The Chairman of the Board holds a standing invitation to all founder gatherings. The board seat structure — and the independent director search — is recorded in the minutes below. For the chairman's own letters, see the Dear Chairman series.

MIN-001· T+49d · Meeting No. 1 Minutes

Minutes of the Board of Directors — Meeting No. 1.

The first meeting of Stratum's board, called to order at 14:03 PT at the Palo Alto offices. Three resolutions adopted: a 90-day pricing pilot with Cordova, a USD 200,000 security audit authorization, and a 0.20% equity top-up for the founding designer. Quorum: 2 of 2 seated directors. Chair pro tempore: Sarah Cannon (Index Ventures).

Prepared by Helm, Secretary pro tempore · Reviewed: S. Cannon, Chair pro tempore · Counsel: J. Karp, Wilson Sonsini
