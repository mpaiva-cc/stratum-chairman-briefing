§ Why Tessera Bank

A bank built by operators, for builders.

Our mission

Tessera Bank exists to put capital in the hands of the people building real things — manufacturers, designers, founders, contractors, growers. We are a chartered financial institution serving 280,000 small and mid-market businesses across the US, EMEA, and LATAM, with $42B in deposits and a 99.97% uptime guarantee. We treat banking as infrastructure, not theatre.

Our culture

We are small teams with real authority. Engineers ship to production on day one. Designers own outcomes, not deliverables. Product managers write code. Recruiters sit on hiring committees. We hold each other to the standard of the work, not the hours — and we measure what we shipped, not what we attended. There are no committees about committees here.

Benefits & comp

Top-of-band cash compensation benchmarked to Radford P75. Equity in a profitable, growing bank. Comprehensive health, dental, and vision in every country we hire. 28 days paid leave plus your birthday off. Full remote optionality on most roles. $3,000 annual home-office stipend. Real parental leave — 20 weeks, fully paid, for any parent.
